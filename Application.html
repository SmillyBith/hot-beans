Job specifications and Requirements

As part of the Hot Beans Website there are multiple jobs that you will do as part of our team. When joining our team you will start to get to learn HTML, C# and Javascript and the advanced basics of what to do and how to do it. Once you have completed your 8 weeks of training you will then be able to build some of the customers websites, the websites will be less complicated but to the customers standards. After you mostly know how to use these different types of programming languages.

The requiments to be able join are company is to have a qualifications for website development or similar these can be things such as Games design and It security systems. or similar. With these qualifications you are able to apply to join Hot beans see the Online application form for how to join us.
